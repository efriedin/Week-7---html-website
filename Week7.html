Maxine Stewart

An artist's journey to discovering the beauty of an abstract world

About Paintings Sculptures

Maxine Stewart is a young artist born in the year 2000. Born and raised rural Illinois, Maxine combines her life experience, talent, and unique world view to create beautiful abstract art. Her work is truly awe-inspiring. Her use of color, line and form will take you into a world all her own.

Maxine's Education Background:

  • NIU - Bachelor's in Fine Arts
  • College of Dupage
  • Elgin Community College - Associate in Art
  • Illinois Valley Community College
